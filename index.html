Délabré

    This photo essay seeks to explore the interplay between human structures and the natural world. When manmade structures are left unattended, unkept and largely forgotten, nature emerges to retake her own.

    Here, we examine the destruction of an edifice by human behavior (or lack of action) as compared to destruction by the natural world as it consumes the corpses of forgotten and abandoned buildings.

    The tension between the tragic and the beautiful - of the lost and the reborn - creates a dissonance that provokes the mind.

    All photos in this gallery were taken in the Central Virginia region in April 2024.
